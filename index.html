About Us

Our Mission

SoftQuest is a vibrant, fully indigenous Information Technology company, incorporated to provide unmatched professional IT services to Companies, Enterprises and Organizations nationwide. We provide superior services in the areas of corporate training, e-business consulting, as well as development, procurement, implementation, maintenance, and support of IT solutions and infrastructures.
We have remained relevant over the past decade by being proactively flexible to serve the desires of today’s customers, while anticipating the market of tomorrow. We consistently find the groove of momentum by aligning ourselves with the global competition and information technology that is morphing at warp speed.
Our vigor lies not only in the pool of highly experienced and dedicated professionals we have at our disposal but also in a system that ensures we continue to attract the best the industry has to offer. We can boast of over fifty (50) years combined managerial experience of delivering first solutions to multinational organizations, government and Para – government agencies and many major economic players.
